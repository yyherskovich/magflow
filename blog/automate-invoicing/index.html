Stop Chasing Payments: Automate Your Invoicing

February 2026
← Back to all posts

Finishing the layout does not mean the issue is finished.

Invoices still need to go out. Payments need to be collected. Customers may need reminders. If that process is manual, money can easily be delayed.

PublicationFlow helps publishers connect production with billing.

Once ad reservations are in the system, that information can be used for billing instead of being typed again somewhere else. You can export bills to QuickBooks or Xero, helping reduce duplicate work and billing mistakes.

It also supports payment workflows, including secure credit-card charging through saved tokens. That makes it easier to collect payment while keeping sensitive card information protected.

For busy publication offices, this can make a real difference.

Instead of asking, "Who still needs to pay?" or "Did this invoice get sent?" your team can work from a more organized system.

PublicationFlow also helps with customer communication, including payment reminders and other advertiser emails.

Better billing means better cash flow, fewer missed invoices, and less time spent chasing payments after every issue.

If invoicing is still a separate end-of-deadline scramble, PublicationFlow can help make it part of the process.
